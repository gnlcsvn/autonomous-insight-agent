# Topic ideas (editable)

Add any topic you want the scout to consider. One idea per bullet, starting with `- `.
You can add an optional note or source hint after a `:`.

**How it's used:** each day the scout pools candidates from this list AND from its fresh radar,
and weights them **equally**. Being on this list does NOT make a topic win. The pick is decided by
the **real data check** (which topic has the strongest, most-sourced, freshest quantifiable finding),
with a tiebreak for staying diverse from recent drafts. Items already drafted/published are skipped
automatically (tracked in `state/used-topics.json`), so you don't need to delete used ones.
If nothing here is new, the scout just uses the radar.
